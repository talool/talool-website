Fundraising With Talool

Printing, shipping, and accounting for paper coupon books is a real pain when you're trying to run a successful fundraiser. It is easy to see how going digital could improve the situation, but many groups have concerns about how to make that transition successfully. That's where we come in. Our online tools can help you get your digital fundraiser up and running quickly. Our Pre-Paid Access and In-App Purchase options give you two ways to integrate our mobile apps into your existing fundraiser process. And, our innovative communication tools make initiating the fundraiser as easy as sending an email.

Benefits include:

Growing Sales
Knowing your customer base is the first step to growing your sales. With Talool you can always reach the users who have purchased the mobile version of your book, so each year's sales benefits from the year before it.
In-App Payments -- Get Paid Right Away!
We've integrated with Braintree (a property of PayPal) to ensure your customers have a world-class payment experience, and you'll get the money you are raising as quickly as possible. When a customer purchases a digital book in our apps, your proceeds are deposited into your bank account within 2 days. If you need to know who made each sale, we give you the tracking tools to make that possible too.
Pre-Paid Access Codes
If you aren't ready to go 100% digital yet, you can sell paper coupon books AND access to digital books in the Talool apps at the same time. We give you the ability to generate as many Access Codes as you like, so you can offer pre-paid access to your content in our apps.
Social Graph
Our Facebook integration and mobile email tools can make every book sale lead to many, many more. Each time a customer buys a book in our app we encourage them to share their support with their Facebook friends and their email contacts.
