About Payback Mobile

The Payback book was founded in 2001 on a simple three part philosophy:

  • To help local schools raise much needed money with an easy and profitable fundraiser.
  • To offer area merchants a LOCAL, "neighborhood marketing" strategy that also helps in their goal of supporting their community.
  • To save families money CLOSE TO HOME while eating out, shopping, going to movies, visiting local attraction and more!

Payback's founder, Ted Shamp, spent 12 years with Entertainment Publications, (publishers of the Entertainment and Gold C coupon books) most recently as Regional Manager -- Fundraising Division for Oregon/Idaho/Hawaii.

Upon leaving his position with Entertainment, Ted saw the need for a Vancouver area discount book... one that allowed families to save money at HOME -- not after driving a half hour to Portland.

It was also decided that Payback would give a generous 50% profit ($10 of the $20 purchase price) for every Payback Mobile App that a school sells...an unheard of concept in coupon book fundraising (other similar books start at a mere 20% profit).

It was this willingness to develop a true "50/50 partnership" with the local schools, while generating new customers for area merchants, that led to the success of the Payback and its place today as the favorite choice for Bend area families looking for new businesses to visit and new ways to save money!
