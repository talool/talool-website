Platform Benefits

Eliminate printing costs
You pay more than just the printer when publish a paper coupon book. And the consignment distribution model forces you to print, ship, and store many more books than you ever sell. Wouldn't it be nice to eliminate or even reduce those costs. Talool can help you transition away from that waste and improve your margins.

Shorter time to market
It takes a lot of time to design, proof, and print your books. And if you want to make a change, or add a new merchant or deal after you've gone to print, you're out of luck until you do it all over with the next year's book. With Talool it's easy to add new merchants and deals any time throughout the year.

Easier sales process for you and your customers
You start over each year selling your books. You lose a lot of happy customers, and are forced try to replace them with a new group. Unless you sign up more schools or fundraising organizations, your sales will stay flat. Why limit sales to your direct fundraising efforts when you can open up the next level of indirect sales through your users actions on the Talool Platform. With Talool you can always reach the users who have purchase the mobile version of you book, so each year's sales benefits from the year before it. Plus we give you the online tools needed to support the efforts of the schools and organizations you work with. And when users redeem and share your deals, their friends are encouraged to get their own copy of your book.

Powerful analytics
Merchants want the ability to measure the success of the offers you distribute. With Talool you'll create deals that are more than just marketing; We power deals that help you learn about existing customers and identify the new customers waiting to be discovered.
