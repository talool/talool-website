Help Support Your Local Schools

Talool works with the schools in your area to improve their annual fundraisers. In the past, Parent Teacher Organizations have sold paper coupon books and only received 50% of the proceeds to support their schools. We feel they should get % of the proceeds.

If you agree and would like to help the schools in your community, please consider contributing one or more coupons to your business. If you already publish coupons in your community, you can contribute the same offers for this cause. If you don't typically offer discounts, please consider contacting us to find other ways you can help. Marketing to the parents in your community can be a great way to increase the traffic to your business.

If you're interested, please contact us to get started.
