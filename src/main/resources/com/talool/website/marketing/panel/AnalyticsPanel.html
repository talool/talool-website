Talool Tools & Analytics

We've invested a lot of our time and energy to make sure our admin tools can save you even more time and energy. We know it takes a lot to design, proof, and publish deals for paper distribution. The Talool platform gives you visual tools that allow you to push digital deals to customers in moments. And unlike paper, you'll have real-time feedback on who is using the deals you publish, and the ability to immediately make changes to optimize your offers.

Talool Tools include:

Content Wizards
The Talool admin provides easy to use Wizards that give you step by step guidance for managing all the information and images supporting your business and your deals. You'll get to see how your content will appear in the apps before you publish it.
Stock Photos
Talool provides a library of fully licensed stock images that are categorized and optimized for use in our apps. We make it easy to quickly find an image that will make your offer look great. And if you already have images you love, you can quickly upload them to Talool for use in the apps.
Real-time Analytics
We collect a lot of data, and we want you to be able to use it. Whether you're looking for trends in customer behavior, the details of a recent coupon redemption, or the name of a past customer, our tools will make it easy to find what you need.
Fundraiser Tracking
When you use our platform to support a fundraiser, we give you tools to track your sales back to a specific organization or member of an organization. Monitoring the performance of your fundraiser in real-time gives you powerful information to help maximize the money you help raise.
