White Label: Your Brand Powered By Talool

Talool makes it easy to get all the benefits of our platform, and still promote your brand. You give us an app icon, color palette, and a few images, and we'll take care of the rest. When we're done, you'll have a licensed version of our apps available in Apple's App Store and in Google Play. We'll also manage the hard stuff like app updates and scaling servers to ensure your customers get a great experience.

White Label service includes:

App Design & Development
The iOS and Android apps will be updated to include your branding, design, and color palette.
Facebook Integration & Development
We will host all the web and mobile-web pages that are needed to support our integration with Facebook, and we'll help you get required approvals from Facebook to support this through your own Facebook application.
App/Play Store Setup
We help you create accounts in the iTunes App Store and Google Play Store. Talool will act as your agent and help you navigate all the steps necessary for testing and publishing the apps.
Payment Customization
We will route in-app purchases to a Braintree account owned by you.
iOS & Android Updates
We will update your white label apps once a year, and as needed when major changes are made by Apple and Google.
Fully Managed Cloud Infrastructure
We will ensure that all the hardware and software supporting your apps are running optimally and always available.
Talool Tools
And, of course, you will have access to all the standard tools and analytics available on our platform.
