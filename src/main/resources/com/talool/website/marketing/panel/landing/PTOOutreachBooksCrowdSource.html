It Starts With An Email

You just need to email your organization to install the Talool app and make a purchase with an assigned tracking code. All the door-to-door selling is gone, because the same instructions apply to selling to friends and family.

Here's an example of that email.
It's time again for our annual fundraiser, and this year we are offering a wonderful collection of discounts to many of the local businesses you visit every week. There are over $500 in savings available in this collection which is available for only $20. To show your support of [Your Organization] please install the Talool App and purchase the [Book Name]. Don't forget to enter our tracking code, Q6DJHW, when you make your purchase, so 90% of the proceeds can be sent to [Your Organization].

Please remember to thank all the local business who participated.

Each Purchase Drives More Sales

Each customer who buys a coupon book on your behalf is delivered a special message encouraging them to email their friends and family to do the same. In addition, customers who signed in to Talool with Facebook have those same instructions posted to their Facebook page. Your fundraiser will reach many more people than you ever imagined.
